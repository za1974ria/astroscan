To get the fastest response, please use a clear subject-line prefix:

|---|---|

| `[RESEARCH]` | Academic collaboration, citations, joint papers |

## What to include in your message

For fastest turnaround:

1. **Who you are** — name, organization, role
2. **What you need** — interview, quote, screenshot, partnership pitch, citation
3. **Deadline** — if any
4. **Language preference** — French or English
5. **Best contact method** — email, video call, signal, etc.
