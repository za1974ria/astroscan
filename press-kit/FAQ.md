# FAQ · Press Questions

Common questions from journalists and partners. *Questions fréquentes des journalistes et partenaires.*

---

## 🇬🇧 English

### Q: Is ASTRO-SCAN affiliated with NASA, ESA, or any space agency?
**A:** No. ASTRO-SCAN is **independent**. It uses public open data and APIs published by NASA, NOAA, ESA, JPL, CelesTrak, Harvard MicroObservatory, and N2YO under their respective open-data terms. There is no commercial, contractual, or institutional affiliation.

### Q: Who built ASTRO-SCAN?
**A:** Zakaria Chohra, a single independent engineer based in Tlemcen, Algeria. The entire stack — backend, AI orchestration, orbital mechanics, frontend, infrastructure, bilingual content — was built solo.

### Q: Is it really free?
**A:** Yes. No registration, no paywall, no advertising, no third-party trackers, no email harvesting. Free under **CC BY-NC-SA 4.0** for education, research, citizen science, and public outreach. Commercial use requires explicit written permission.

### Q: How is it funded?
**A:** Self-funded by the founder. Hosted on Hetzner Cloud at the founder's expense. The platform is open to **partnerships, grants, and institutional sponsorship** — contact zakaria.chohra@gmail.com.

### Q: Can journalists use the screenshots?
**A:** Yes, freely under CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 with attribution to *Zakaria Chohra · ASTRO-SCAN · astroscan.space*. High-resolution versions in [`./screenshots/`](./screenshots/).

### Q: Can researchers cite ASTRO-SCAN in publications?
**A:** Absolutely. Suggested citation:
> Chohra, Z. (2026). *ASTRO-SCAN: A real-time open-data space observatory.* Tlemcen, Algeria. https://astroscan.space

### Q: Is the source code open?
**A:** ASTRO-SCAN is licensed CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 (free for non-commercial use with share-alike). Code repository access is available on request for institutional partners and researchers — contact the founder.

### Q: What languages does ASTRO-SCAN support?
**A:** French and English natively (cookie-persistent, hreflang-aware sitemap). Arabic content planned for a future release.

### Q: Does it work on mobile?
**A:** Yes — responsive UI, PWA-installable on Android, screenshots in [`./screenshots/`](./screenshots/).

### Q: What about privacy?
**A:** No third-party trackers. Server-side proxy for all external API keys (zero leakage to the browser). Visitor analytics are aggregated and anonymized.
